What I do

Web Design

My Process

I have an intricate process that I go through to make my sites. I ask you a few questions about your business, do some research and I’m on my way. I send you some low fidelity wireframes of the site, and we review them until you are satisfied. I then go on to make a higher fidelity mockup, and recieve more feedback from you. I may make a prototype if necessary, but often, it’s off to the races!

Specialties

As a designer and developer, I specialize in UI(User Interface) Design. My primary experience is in sites for businesses, but I have worked with web applications before, and am willing to do so. I focus on designing for the future. I follow trends that other companies are moving to, while keeping an original flare that makes my sites look like no others out there. Although true that I never recieved proper education for my design, I have lots of experience, and have gone through many trials and trebulations to get to where I am.

Web Development

For Businesses

As a primary UI Designer, my main focus is building websites for businesses. It is important for every business to be online, since so people use the internet. My goal is to make sure your business is able to catch the attention of those people. I create a unique website that grabs customers’ attention as well as easily conveying what you do and gets them to use your business.

More Developer Information

As a developer, I work with many tools every day. My primary focus is on HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and making good websites for businesses. For Javascript, my primary experience is with Vanilla, byt I am using more Vue, as I feel that it is a perfect framework do to its lightwieight libraries and all of its customization. For CSS, I prefer Sass due to the incredible amount of extra functionality it gives to CSS. A developer has to learn more every day, as I do, and love doing so.

Helping Your Business

Being Found Online

Today, it is a necessity that your business has a strong online presence. A good website is a huge part of that, but not always enough. Basic search engine optimization can help by a lot. I run through a checklist to make sure your are ready for Google. I also connect you to services like Google My Business, so that your business is listed on Google, and so that people can see reviews, pictures, and more.

DevOps and More

When a website is created, it needs to be put on a server so that your customers can access it. My primary servers are shared, but I can also set up VPS and dedicated. I also make sure that everyone at your business has an email address that uses your domain’s name, keeping your emails simple and professional. I can set up a custom domain for you and connect it to your server. I handle all of the tricky parts of running a website so you don’t have to.

Pricing

The Value of My Work

Creating a website is an intricate process. It requires research, brainstroming, creating a design, making that design into code, and setting up the site. Often times, things need to be revised and sometimes, completely remade. A lot goes on on my end. This work can take a long time and is quite a bit to do. Because of this, I set up a pricing method that is fair to me and values my work. At the same time, I strive to make my services affordable and just, so that you have a great website at a great price.

My Pricing Structure

So how do I charge for my work, then? For the creation of the site, I use a fixed rate strategy. This assures that no matter how long it takes to complete the site, the price is equal to the amount of work that needs to be done. Standard shared servers are on me, and I charge a yearly fee for the domain, which depends on what domain you would prefer. If you would like to learn more, please get in touch with me, as I am happy to answer any of your questions and would love to work with you.
